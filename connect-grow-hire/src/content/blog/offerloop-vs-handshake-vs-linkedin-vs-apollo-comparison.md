## Handshake: Strengths and Limitations for Networking

Handshake is a popular platform among college students for accessing job postings and internships directly from employers. It excels in this area, providing a tailored experience for students to find opportunities that align with their career goals. However, Handshake falls short when it comes to cold outreach. The platform lacks tools for finding email addresses or personalizing outreach messages, limiting students to interactions initiated by employers.

### Strengths
- Direct access to job and internship postings.
- Tailored specifically for college students.
- Free access for students.

### Limitations
- No tools for cold outreach.
- Limited networking features beyond job applications.
- Dependent on employer-posted opportunities.

## LinkedIn: Strengths and Limitations for Students

LinkedIn is the go-to platform for professional networking, boasting a vast user base and a range of features for building a professional profile. However, its utility for students seeking cold outreach opportunities is limited. LinkedIn's connection request limits and InMail costs can be prohibitive. Moreover, the platform's algorithm often deprioritizes students, making it harder to gain visibility without a substantial existing network.

### Strengths
- Extensive professional network.
- Robust tools for profile building.
- Access to a wide range of industry professionals.

### Limitations
- Connection request limits.
- Costly InMail feature.
- Algorithm may deprioritize student profiles.
- No built-in email extraction tools.

## Apollo: Strengths and Limitations for Students

Apollo is primarily designed for sales teams, offering extensive features for lead generation and sales prospecting. While it provides powerful email finding and AI-powered outreach capabilities, its complex UI and pricing structure are not optimized for college students. The platform is not tailored for informational interviews or student-to-professional networking, making it less suitable for those looking to build industry connections.

### Strengths
- Strong email finding capabilities.
- AI-powered outreach tools.
- Large contact database.

### Limitations
- Overwhelming for non-sales users.
- Not student-friendly pricing.
- Not optimized for informational interviews.
