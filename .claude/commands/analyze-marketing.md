## PHASE 2: PLATFORM AUDIT — X / TWITTER

### Presence
- Handle, follower count, verification, bio copy, link strategy
- Posting frequency (tweets per day/week)
- Ratio of original tweets vs replies vs retweets vs quote tweets

### Content Strategy
- Content pillars with estimated % breakdown (thought leadership, product updates, engagement bait, threads, memes, industry commentary, founder-led)
- Thread strategy — do they publish long-form threads? Structure? Frequency?
- Real-time engagement — do they respond to trends, news, competitor mentions?
- Community interaction — reply speed, tone, do they ratio competitors or go viral in replies?

### What Works / What Doesn't
- Top 3-5 performing tweets (highest engagement) — quote the hook, explain why it worked
- Bottom performers — what patterns flop?
- Hashtag strategy (or lack thereof)

## PHASE 4: PLATFORM AUDIT — TIKTOK

### Presence
- Handle, follower count, total likes, bio, link-in-bio strategy
- Posting frequency, average views per video

### Content Strategy
- Content pillars with % breakdown (product demos, tutorials, behind-the-scenes, trend-jacking, founder-led, UGC, memes, storytelling)
- Video format: talking head, screen recording, b-roll montage, text-on-screen, skit, voiceover?

### Video Deep Dive (CRITICAL)
For their top 5-10 TikToks:
- **Hook** (first 1-3 seconds): Quote exact words + describe visual. Classify: question hook, bold claim, curiosity gap, pattern interrupt, controversy, visual hook
- **Pacing**: Fast cuts (1-2s) vs medium (3-5s) vs slow cinematic? Cut frequency?
- **Structure**: Hook → Problem → Solution → CTA? Front-loaded value or slow build?
- **Audio**: Trending sounds, original audio, voiceover, music choice?
- **Text overlays**: Prominent? Animated? Captions always/sometimes/never?
- **Production quality**: iPhone raw, mid-tier, professional? Which level performs best?
- **CTA style**: Soft, hard, none, "follow for part 2", comment prompt?
- **Average video length** and which lengths perform best

### Performance Patterns
- What topics/formats get the most views?
- What consistently underperforms?
- Are they using TikTok Shop, LIVE, Series, or other features?

## PHASE 6: EMAIL MARKETING AUDIT

- Do they use a dedicated newsletter platform (Substack, Beehiiv, ConvertKit) or in-house?

### Email Content & Strategy
Search for: their newsletter archives, email screenshots on social media, reviews of their emails, and any public-facing email content.
- Email types: promotional, educational, product updates, onboarding sequences, abandoned cart, re-engagement, event invites, weekly/monthly digest
- Sending frequency
- Subject line patterns — length, personalization, emoji usage, urgency/curiosity hooks
- Preview text strategy

### Design & Copy
- Email design style: minimal text, image-heavy, HTML designed, plain text?
- Copy tone — does it match their social voice or shift?
- CTA placement and style (single CTA vs multiple, button vs text link)
- Personalization signals (name, behavior-based, segmentation evidence)

### Funnel Integration
- How does email connect to their social media? Cross-promotion?
- Do they drive social followers to email or vice versa?
- Evidence of automation sequences (welcome series, drip campaigns)
- Retargeting signals

## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

1. Search for all social media profiles across X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram
2. Analyze their most recent 20-30 posts per platform
3. Search for viral or notable content from the past 6-12 months
4. Check Meta Ad Library for active campaigns
5. Search for their newsletter/email signup and any archived emails
6. Search for press coverage or case studies about their marketing
7. Look for creator/influencer collaborations
8. Cross-reference engagement rates and growth where possible

Be specific. Use real examples. Quote actual hooks. Reference actual posts. The analysis should be detailed enough to serve as a blueprint.
