## What a cold email to a Goldman analyst should look like

Here's a real email, the kind Offerloop generates for you in about ten seconds, then lets you edit:

```
Subject: USC Marshall '28, quick question about Goldman TMT

Hi Priya,

I'm a USC Marshall sophomore studying finance, saw you moved from Marshall
into Goldman's TMT group, which is exactly the path I'm trying to figure out.

Would you be open to a 15-minute meeting the week of October 6th? I have
a few specific questions about TMT recruiting and how you approached it from USC.

Thanks for considering,
Daniel
```

Three rules baked into it: the USC connection in line one, one specific career detail in line two, a bounded 15-minute ask in line three. Under 80 words so it reads fully in the preview pane.
