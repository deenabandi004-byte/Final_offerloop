## How does the cold email generator work?

You give it three things, who you're emailing, who you are, and one detail you found about them. It returns a four-sentence email built on the structure that actually gets replies: shared connection first, specific reference second, bounded 15-minute ask third. You edit the personalization line and send.

## What makes this different from using ChatGPT?

ChatGPT writes a generic, often over-long email that reads like a generic, over-long email, and analysts spot it instantly. Offerloop's generator is tuned on what gets replies in recruiting specifically: the right length, the school-connection lead, the bounded ask, no flattery padding. It also plugs into the rest of Offerloop, so the same tool that drafts the email can also find the person's verified email address.
