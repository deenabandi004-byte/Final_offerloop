Most candidates don't fail on technicals; they fail on a generic "why Goldman" and visibly fading energy by the third interview. By the time you have a superday booked, you don't have time to read ten interview guides. You need to know the format, drill the right questions for *your* group, and lock a specific "why Goldman", fast. Generic prep spreads you thin across the wrong things.
